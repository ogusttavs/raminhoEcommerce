# Prompt Mestre: Morango Hidropônico

Este arquivo documenta os prompts exatos utilizados para gerar as 4 imagens oficiais do Morango Hidropônico.  
Use esses prompts como base se precisar gerar materiais adicionais para manter 100% de coerência visual.

## Parâmetros Globais
- **Produto:** Morango Hidropônico (fresh hydroponic strawberry)
- **Fundo Padrão:** `#F7F3EB` puro (Exceto Lifestyle)
- **Estilo:** Commercial food photography / Hyperrealistic

---

## 01. Hero Shot (Foto Vitrine)
**Arquivo:** `01_morango_hidroponico_hero.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Commercial studio product photography, a single perfect whole hydroponic strawberry (morango) resting on a perfectly clean #F7F3EB studio background. Shot with an 85mm lens, f/8 for sharp edge-to-edge product focus. Diffused softbox lighting from the top-left, very subtle micro water condensation droplets on the skin. Photorealistic, organic textures, hyper-detailed, advertising quality, negative space around the product, like an Apple product launch photo.

## 02. Macro (Textura)
**Arquivo:** `02_morango_hidroponico_macro.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Extreme macro photography of a hydroponic strawberry (morango) skin. Shot with a 100mm macro lens f/2.8. Shallow depth of field. Ultra-detailed organic texture, showing seeds, vibrant natural red colors. Microscopic water condensation droplets magnifying the surface details. Diffused side lighting creating soft highlights. Hyperrealistic, 8k resolution, food magazine cover quality.

## 03. Interior (Corte)
**Arquivo:** `03_morango_hidroponico_interior.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Commercial food photography of a perfectly half-cut hydroponic strawberry (morango), resting on a clean #F7F3EB background. The interior flesh is juicy, vibrant red and white, and perfectly ripe. Shot with an 85mm lens. Diffused lighting highlighting the glistening natural juices inside. Hyperrealistic, flawless studio execution, organic and appetizing.

## 04. Contexto Minimalista (Lifestyle)
**Arquivo:** `04_morango_hidroponico_lifestyle_tabua.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Minimalist culinary photography. A fresh hydroponic strawberry (morango) elegantly placed on a light oak wood cutting board. The background is a soft, blurred #F7F3EB wall. Shot with a 50mm lens f/4. Natural morning sunlight filtering through a window, casting soft, elegant shadows. High-end culinary aesthetic, hyper-realistic, sophisticated, no clutter.
