# Prompt Mestre: Figo Roxo

Este arquivo documenta os prompts exatos planeados para gerar as 4 imagens oficiais do Figo Roxo.  

## Parâmetros Globais
- **Produto:** Figo Roxo Fresco (fresh purple fig)
- **Fundo Padrão:** `#F7F3EB` puro (Exceto Lifestyle)
- **Estilo:** Commercial food photography / Hyperrealistic

---

## 01. Hero Shot (Foto Vitrine)
**Status:** Gerado
**Arquivo:** `01_figo_roxo_hero.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Commercial studio product photography, a single perfect whole fresh purple fig (figo roxo) resting on a perfectly clean #F7F3EB studio background. Shot with an 85mm lens, f/8 for sharp edge-to-edge product focus. Diffused softbox lighting from the top-left, very subtle micro water condensation droplets on the dark purple skin. Photorealistic, organic textures, hyper-detailed, advertising quality, negative space around the product, like an Apple product launch photo.

## 02. Macro (Textura)
**Status:** Gerado
**Arquivo:** `02_figo_roxo_macro.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Extreme macro photography of a fresh purple fig (figo roxo) skin. Shot with a 100mm macro lens f/2.8. Shallow depth of field. Ultra-detailed organic dark purple and slightly green dusty texture. Microscopic water condensation droplets magnifying the surface details. Diffused side lighting creating soft highlights. Hyperrealistic, 8k resolution, food magazine cover quality.

## 03. Interior (Corte)
**Status:** Pendente de geração
**Arquivo:** `03_figo_roxo_interior.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Commercial food photography of a perfectly half-cut purple fig (figo roxo), resting on a clean #F7F3EB background. The interior flesh is juicy, vibrant ruby red and pink, perfectly ripe with visible seeds. Shot with an 85mm lens. Diffused lighting highlighting the glistening natural juices inside. Hyperrealistic, flawless studio execution, organic and appetizing.

## 04. Contexto Minimalista (Lifestyle)
**Status:** Pendente de geração
**Arquivo:** `04_figo_roxo_lifestyle_tabua.png`  
**Prompt original:**
> Minimalist culinary photography. A fresh purple fig (figo roxo) elegantly sliced in half and placed on a light oak wood cutting board. The background is a soft, blurred #F7F3EB wall. Shot with a 50mm lens f/4. Natural morning sunlight filtering through a window, casting soft, elegant shadows. High-end culinary aesthetic, hyper-realistic, sophisticated, no clutter.
